About Me

I'm Dr. Bhavesh Bisen and Homoeopathy Doctor

Dr. Bhavesh completed his MD from R.K.D.F. Homoeopathic Medical College & Hospital, Research Centre, under SRK University Bhopal (M.P.) with a dissertation on "Efficacy of Homoeopathic Medicines in the Treatment of Alopecia with Relation to Thyroid Disorders" under the supervision of Dr. S.K. Sharma, Professor, Department of Practice of Medicine. Previously, he had done his dissertation work on "Effect of Counselling on Blood Sugar Level and BMI of Type II Diabetes" and "Influence of Body Mass Index on Serum Lipid Profile of Patients Visiting Hospital" under the supervision of Dr. Meera Vaidya, Professor & Head, Department of Food & Nutrition, Govt. M.H. College of Home Science & Science for Women (Autonomous), Jabalpur (M.P.) in 2013 and 2014, respectively.

Presently, Dr. Bhavesh is working as an Assistant Professor at Hahnemann Homeopathic Medical College (HHMC), Bhopal (M.P.). He mainly taught, Medicine, Pathology, and Microbiology, Biochemistry, Public Nutrition subjects to Under Graduates and Post Graduates Students. He also guided 1 Student as a Supervisor with the topic “Influence of Body Mass Index on Serum Lipid Profile of Patients Visiting Hospital” in 2014. He had previously worked as an MO at the same college. He also worked as an RMO at M. G. Homoeopathic College and Hospital, Jabalpur (M.P).

He has also worked as a renowned resident homoeopathy practitioner at Koh-e- Fiza, Bhopal (M.P.) since 2012. He is very active on social media platforms like YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and Linkedin for serving people and helping people to prevent themselves from various diseases or cure themselves if they are diseased. He also started 2 YouTube Channels with the names (1) Dr.Bhavesh Sir Classes and (2) Dr.Bhavesh Bisen Homeopathy in order to serve people after the First Wave of COVID-19 in 2020 by giving them knowledge to strengthen their immunity to fight this pandemic and also for chronic and non-chronic diseases.

In addition, Dr. Bhavesh participated in community and social work by helping Special Olympics Bharat during health promotions in May 2009 and October 2012 in Jabalpur (M.P.). He also took part in Swastha Jiwan Shailee Shivir event, which was hosted by Aarog Bharti, Jabalpur (M.P).

Dr. Bisen also participated in a number of workshops and training sessions to enrich his knowledge and gain practical exposure in the fields of Nutrition and Food.
